About Clinton County

Clinton County, Missouri, established in 1833, is located in the northwestern part of the state and is known for its rich agricultural heritage, welcoming communities, and strong sense of local pride. The county seat, Plattsburg, serves as the center of county government and commerce, while the county's towns and rural landscapes reflect a blend of historic charm and modern growth. Conveniently situated near the Kansas City metropolitan area, Clinton County offers residents and visitors the benefits of peaceful small-town living with easy access to major employment, shopping, and entertainment opportunities. From productive farmland and scenic countryside to parks, outdoor recreation, and community events, Clinton County continues to preserve its history while embracing opportunities for the future.
